---
title: End your alt text with a period
tags: [HTML]
description: Use proper punctuation in alt texts for accessibility.
slug: end-your-alt-text-with-a-period
date: 2025-12-01
---

The `alt` attribute in images should use proper punctuation so that _screen readers can describe the image using a more natural rhythm._ Adding a period at the end improves accessibility: it gives a slight pause and helps with readability.

See [Digital Accessibility page at USC](https://sc.edu/about/offices_and_divisions/digital-accessibility/toolbox/best_practices/alternative_text/step-by-step-instructions-alt-text/example-alt-text-steps-in-action/index.php).
