---
title: When multiple arguments are passed to `unshift`, they are inserted as a chunk
tags: [JavaScript]
description: Passing multiple arguments to `unshift` is not what you think
slug: passing-multiple-arguments-to-unshift
postIndex: 6
---

If you pass multiple arguments to `unshift()`, they are not inserted one by one, but as a chunk:

```js
let a = []; // []
a.unshift(1, 2) // [1, 2] -- not [2, 1]
```
